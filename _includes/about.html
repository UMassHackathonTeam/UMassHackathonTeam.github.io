Our Team


The UMass Hackathon Team comprises of students from all over the University who love to build and code.

Our mission is to provide our members with instruction and growth opportunities through workshops, competitions, and networking.

The UMass Hackathon Team is open to any and all students enrolled at the University of Massachusetts Amherst who want to learn and build great things.

We meet weekly on Thursdays from 7-9pm in Marcus Hall 106 and are always open to meet new students with an interest in joining our organization.
